Privacy Policy for Anchor: Feel Time Again

Last updated: December 16, 2024

1. Information We Collect

Anchor: Feel Time Again is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal information:

  • Wellness and mood journaling data - Only stored locally on your device
  • Photos - Only if you choose to use premium photo memory features
  • HealthKit data - Only if you grant permission, stored in iOS Health app
  • Time awareness and grounding data - Personal reflection notes and mindfulness activities, stored locally

2. How We Use Information

  • All data is processed locally on your device
  • HealthKit integration allows mood journaling data to appear in iOS Health app (with your permission)
  • iCloud sync (optional) backs up your data to your personal iCloud account
  • We do not access, collect, or transmit your personal data to third parties
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising technologies are used

3. Data Storage

  • Local storage: All app data is stored securely on your device using iOS CoreData
  • iCloud sync: Optional backup to your personal iCloud account (user controlled)
  • No remote servers: We do not store your data on external servers
  • Encryption: Data is protected using standard iOS encryption

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit your personal data to third parties. Your information remains completely private to you.

5. HealthKit Integration

  • Mood journaling data can be saved to iOS Health app with explicit user permission
  • We only write data you explicitly create through the app's wellness check-in process
  • HealthKit data is governed by Apple's privacy policies and iOS security
  • You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time through iOS Settings

6. Photo Storage (Premium Feature)

  • Photos are stored locally in the app's secure sandbox
  • Photos are included in iCloud backup only if you enable iCloud sync
  • We only access photos you explicitly capture within the app
  • Photos can be deleted individually or all at once through app settings

7. In-App Purchases

  • Premium subscriptions ($0.99/month) are processed entirely by Apple
  • We do not collect or store payment information
  • Purchase data is handled according to Apple's privacy policy
  • Subscription management is through your Apple ID settings

8. Analytics and Tracking

Anchor: Feel Time Again does NOT use:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising networks or tracking pixels
  • Crash reporting services that collect personal data
  • Social media integration that tracks users

9. Your Rights - Data Export and Deletion

  • Export: You can export all your data at any time through app settings
  • Delete: You can delete all data through the app settings
  • Selective deletion: Delete individual mood entries, photos, or time anchors
  • Complete removal: Uninstalling the app removes all local data

10. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. International Users

This app is designed to comply with privacy regulations including GDPR (European Union) and CCPA (California) through local data processing and user control.

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

13. App Purpose and Wellness Disclaimer

Anchor: Feel Time Again is designed for personal wellness journaling and mindfulness practice. It is intended for emotional awareness and personal reflection only. This app is not intended for medical use, diagnosis, or treatment and should not replace professional care when needed.
